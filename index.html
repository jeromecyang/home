about

Thank you for visiting my website! My name is Jerome Yang. I'm a software developer based in Boston, MA, and also a frequent traveler, a culture/social observer and a blogger (blog.jeromeyang.com). As a Taiwanese immigrant to the US, I cherish my identity as a foreigner in tech industry and enjoy the cross-cultural interaction around me everyday.

Educated as a geographer and sociologist in college, working as a software developer was never my goal until grad school, where I saw how information technology can be employed to solve numerous environmental and social issues. This is why after receiving a degree in geographic information science (GIS), I began my career as an engineer at the world's leading geographic information software company Esri, completed coursework in algorithms and big data analytics, and currently work at a customer journey analytics startup Pointillist.

I'm excited about travel, cross-cultural experience and linguistics. Having visited 18 countries, I've been increasingly interested in building products that connect people and help people communicate across cultures Computer linguistics, machine translation and human language understanding are areas I'd like to dive into. Meanwhile, creating user interfaces with succinct information and rich graphics continues to be my primary area of expertise.

Languages that I speak:

  • Intermediate - Hokkien, Python
  • Beginner - Spanish, Japanese
